About

Open the app overview, usage guidance and important note.

This dashboard is designed to highlight the health behaviours that matter most across recovery, nutrition, activity and mental wellbeing. It is not an optimisation tool. Its purpose is to provide clear, evidence-informed guidance that helps you focus on the habits most likely to improve energy, resilience, health and quality of life.

How to Use

Each tab contains either a set of key behaviours or grouped sub-categories. Every item has a weight that reflects its relative contribution within that category. The app provides two reference points for self-assessment:

  • Optimal: the strongest evidence-based target for that behaviour.
  • 80/20: a simpler, more practical standard that captures most of the benefit.

Enter a score from 0 to 100 for each behaviour based on how closely your current habits match the guidance. The app multiplies your score by each item’s weight, totals the results for the category, and then combines category scores into the overall score. Use the overall tab to spot the largest opportunities first.

Important Note

The information in this app is for general educational purposes only. It is not personalised medical, nutritional or psychological advice. Individual needs vary, so consult an appropriately qualified professional before making major changes to your diet, exercise, recovery or lifestyle habits.
