About

Open the app overview, usage guidance and important note.

This dashboard is designed to highlight the health behaviours that matter most across recovery, mental health, activity and nutrition. Its purpose is to cut through fashionable but low-value advice and point attention toward the evidence-based habits most likely to improve energy, resilience, physical function, long-term health and quality of life.

How to Use

Each tab contains either a set of key behaviours or grouped sub-categories. Every item has a weight that reflects its relative contribution within that category based on practical importance, not trendiness. The app provides two reference points for self-assessment:

  • Optimal: a strong evidence-based target associated with better health, function or resilience.
  • 80/20: a simpler, more achievable standard that still captures much of the benefit for most people.

Enter a score from 0 to 100 for each behaviour based on how closely your current habits match the guidance. The app multiplies your score by each item’s weight, totals the results for the category, and then combines category scores into the overall score. Use the overall tab to identify the changes most likely to improve both daily vitality and broader health outcomes first.

Important Note

The information in this app is for general educational purposes only. It is not personalised medical, nutritional or psychological advice, and it does not diagnose or treat illness. Individual needs vary, so consult an appropriately qualified professional before making major changes to your diet, exercise, recovery, workload or lifestyle habits.
